The deliverables

Two documents. Every detail covered.

Business Plan

30 sections · ~35 pages
  • Mission, Vision & Concept Overview
  • Location Strategy & Environment
  • Brand Positioning & Identity
  • Full menu with dishes & ingredients
  • Beverage program (hot, cold, alcohol)
  • Equipment & staffing model
  • Daily programming by time of day
  • Target guests & SWOT analysis
  • Operations & POS systems

Financial Model

12 pages · landscape format
  • Funding structure & equity split
  • Opening budget breakdown (building, equipment, preopening)
  • Methodology for sales & cost assumptions
  • Weekly covers & sales by day/period
  • Wages & operating cost breakdown
  • Year 1 income statement (13 periods)
  • 5-year projections with growth scenarios
  • Balance sheet & cash position
  • Ramp-up modeling built in
  • All numbers deterministic, never hallucinated
